Lion

Their tongues have sharp-pointed rasps, called papillae, which are used to scrape meat off the bones.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Length: 2.1 metres
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Thick bush

Nearly all wild lions live in Africa, below the Sahara Desert, but one small population exists around Gir Forest National Park in western India.

Tiger

Unlike most other cats, tigers are great swimmers and actually like the water.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 3.3 metres
  • Average Lifespan: 26 years
  • Habitat: Savannah

Tigers communicate using scent markings, visual signals and lots of sounds like roars, growls, snarls, grunts, moans, mews and hisses.
